What Makes Sandals Special

True All-Inclusive Luxury: Unlike many "all-inclusive" resorts with hidden costs, Sandals genuinely includes everything. Unlimited premium liquor, gourmet dining at up to 16 restaurants per property, water sports, scuba diving for certified divers, land sports including pickleball, entertainment, tips, and even airport transfers. Budget with confidence - the rate you pay is your total expense (beyond souvenirs and spa upgrades).

Couples-Only Romance: Sandals pioneered couples-only resorts, creating an adult atmosphere free from family vacation chaos. Whether you're honeymooners, celebrating an anniversary, or simply escaping together, the environment enhances romance while pickleball adds active fun.

Planning Your Visit

Best Time to Visit

Peak Season (December - April): Perfect weather across the Caribbean with minimal rain and lower humidity. Prices are highest but conditions are ideal. Book 6-12 months in advance for the best rates and room selection.

Shoulder Season (May & November): Excellent value with good weather. May offers post-spring break calm before summer, while November provides pre-Christmas pricing with improving weather after hurricane season.

Summer/Fall (June - October): Lowest prices of the year. Hurricane season brings risk (Sandals offers excellent rebooking policies), but many days are beautiful with brief afternoon showers. Great for budget-conscious couples willing to accept some weather uncertainty.

Getting There

Each Sandals location is served by an international airport with direct flights from major North American cities. Sandals includes roundtrip airport transfers with every booking - you'll be greeted at the airport and whisked to the resort by private car or bus. Jamaica's Montego Bay (MBJ) and Nassau, Bahamas (NAS) offer the most flight options.

Choosing Your Resort

When selecting a Sandals property for pickleball, confirm court availability as the brand continues expanding pickleball across its portfolio. Contact Sandals directly or check the resort's amenity list. Many guests use Sandals' "Stay at 1, Play at 2" program, splitting their stay between two resorts on the same island to experience different properties while maintaining pickleball access.

What's Included

Your Sandals stay includes: unlimited gourmet dining at up to 16 restaurants per resort serving Caribbean, French, Italian, Asian, and steakhouse cuisines; premium liquor and cocktails at multiple bars; pickleball and all land sports; water sports including kayaking, paddleboarding, snorkeling, and sailing; scuba diving (certified divers); fitness center and classes; nightly entertainment; WiFi; airport transfers; tips and gratuities. Not included: spa services (though hydrotherapy and fitness are included) and off-resort excursions.
